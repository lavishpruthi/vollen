About

An independent edit.

The Jewellery Edit is a small, independent publication that curates jewellery finds from Amazon India. We are not a brand, a retailer or a manufacturer — we are readers and writers who love beautiful pieces and want to share the ones worth knowing about.

Every piece featured is hand-picked. We choose for craft, finish, wearability and value. When a piece doesn't earn its place in our own jewellery box, it doesn't make the edit.

How we make money

We participate in the Amazon Associates programme. When you click through one of our links and buy something on Amazon, we may earn a small commission. It costs you nothing extra, and it keeps this edit running. Read our full disclosure.

Not affiliated

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or representing any of the brands whose products we feature. All trademarks and brand names remain the property of their respective owners. Product details, pricing and availability are managed by Amazon and the respective sellers.
