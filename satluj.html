01 The Case

Satluj is built around a real investigation. Human rights activist Jaswant Singh Khalra set out to find his missing aunt — and instead uncovered a system of enforced disappearances and extrajudicial killings carried out by Punjab Police during the insurgency of the early 1990s. The film follows that search as it grows from a personal loss into a reckoning with state power.

Diljit Dosanjh plays Khalra with what several critics have called restraint rather than heroics — a man whose defiance is quiet, procedural, and stubborn rather than theatrical. Arjun Rampal, Suvinder Vicky, and Geetika Vidya Ohlyan round out an ensemble built around the toll that investigation takes on everyone near it.

02 Case Log — The Fight to Release It

2023

Originally titled Ghallughara, the film is completed and submitted for certification, with release planned for the year.

2023–2025

Retitled Punjab 95. The Central Board of Film Certification demands extensive cuts before it will grant a certificate. The filmmakers refuse to release a version altered to that degree, and a theatrical release is shelved.

Jul 2026

The film finally reaches audiences, retitled Satluj, streaming on ZEE5.

48 hrs later

Director Honey Trehan says he was informed the film would be pulled from the platform just over two days after it went live — a decision he has since spoken about publicly as a case study in censorship and the Streisand effect.
